Nau mai - Welcome to OneAction

Purpose

OneAction is a member-driven political organisation, which, through participatory processes, exists to give its members a voice in the decisions which shape Aotearoa New Zealand, and to enact that voice by being elected to parliament by its members

Intro

OneAction challenges ordinary kiwis to take responsibility for the direction of our country and we’re building a digital democracy platform that will allow us to do just that - a platform to give our members a real voice in how we shape Aotearoa New Zealand

Our vision is that by 2030 OneAction will have the support of at least 500,000 New Zealanders, and that for our members, taking part in setting New Zealand’s direction will be a fun and rewarding part of everyday life. And that grassroots political participation will become a new norm in New Zealand, with OneAction leading the charge to ultimately make New Zealand a world example of a new kind of politics for the many not the few

OneAction is going to show that it only takes one simple action, less than 2 minutes each month, from everyday kiwis to start transforming our stale political system

A socially-sustainable political co-operative, the first of its kind in the world, OneAction is not a traditional political party. Powered and built by people from the ground up. No big funders. No external interests. Transparent and Open

It’s still early days but feel free to take a look around and get in contact if you like what you see

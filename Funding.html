Funding

OneAction is a grassroots, people-powered organisation. This is how we deal with money and transparency:

  • We don’t have big donors or external influencers

  • Membership is free

  • Individual members can donate up to a maximum monthly limit. This means one member can't donate significantly more than another. Growing funding relies on growing the membership.

  • OneAction provides total transparency of every dollar in and every dollar out and you can view this online

  • Contributions will only open once we reach Stage 1 (TallyUp 25k)

  • We don’t spend money on public advertising. Our advertsing is by word of mouth.
    (We will spend money on print material to help our members share the vision)

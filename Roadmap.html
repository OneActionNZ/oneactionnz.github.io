Roadmap

OneAction is building from the bottom up, on a shoestring, we don’t have big funders or sponsors. What we do have is a vision to transform politics in New Zealand and a roadmap to get there. Each stage is marked by a milestone in the TallyUp (the number of active members).
Learn about the TallyUp here.

Stage 1 - Vision
TallyUp: 0
Max Donation/Month: $0

This is ground zero where it’s all about spreading the word and growing support for OneAction’s vision

There's always going to be plenty of people who think that what we're doing is impossible - we're not looking to convince them, we're just seeking those who do want to cut a new path, the innovators, the early adopters, the ones who dream big

The Vision stage will run until the TallyUp reaches 25,000. No donations are accepted during this stage. Participation will just be the TallyUp and no doubt we'll start having informal get-togethers to share ideas. Join now here.

Stage 2 - Start-up
TallyUp: 25,000
Max Donation/Month: $5

In this stage we'll establish a core volunteer team and begin more detailed planning

In this stage donations will open with a maximum monthly limit of $5 per member. Learn more about donation limits here

Donations will be used to start estabilshing basic systsms and processes, enabling meetings and travel. Remember every dollar in and every dollar out is completly trasparent and viewable online in the open ledger

The Start-up stage runs until the TallyUp reaches 50,000

Stage 3 - Build
TallyUp: 50,000
Max Donation/Month: $10

This is where the real work begins. In the Build stage we'll create the digital democracy platform and policy framework. Members are verified to get ready for secure online participation and voting. Lean more about our plans for the OneAction Digital Democracy Platform and Policy Framework here

A lot of work has already been done around the world in various small projects to create some of the online tools that we'll need but make no mistake, this is poineering sutff and building our platform is going to be a huge task. The expectation is that there will be a mix of voluntry and paid work

The Build stage runs until the TallyUp reaches 150,000 and our online platform is at stage where we can begin using it for collective decision making

Stage 4 - Quorum
TallyUp: 150,000
Max Donation/Month: $15

In Quorum stage we launch our digital democracy platform and members start actively participating.

The first rounds of participation will focus on setting the long-term values of the collective, and giving us an opportunity to update our constitution, should we decide to do so

Stage 5 - Launch

TallyUp: 200,000
Max Donation/Month: $15

Game On! With the support of 200,000 of New Zealands OneAction will register as an official New Zealand political party. We will make history by being the first modern party to enter parliament on our first run.

Yes, you read that right, OneAction is tuning politics on its head, we will only register as an official political party once we are prepared with a solid policy set and have the support we need to take seats in parliament.
